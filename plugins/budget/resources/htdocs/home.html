The Hutt City Budget and Advocacy Service is a voluntary community service that helps you get out of money trouble.
Call us on 04 5666357
Our mission is to help borrowers to become budgeters and budgeters become savers.
Wherever you are on this journey we may be able to help you. You can make an appointment to see one of our mentors, or you can use the self-help tools in this website, just signup.

Being in control of your money is most often about having a good understanding of your income and expenses, while making provision for the unexpected moments in life when an unplanned event happens for which you need money, or for the expenses that occur less frequently. Most people not only need a budget, but also a cash flow view for a year.

There are many ways our mentors may be able to help you:

  • Preparing budgets and cash flow forecast.
  • Advice on debt consolidation and debt repayment relief.
  • Finding allowances and/or benefits you may not be aware are available.
  • Assisting with hardship applications.
  • Even advising on debt write off.
Our service can assist you in these matters and everything is confidential.
